
1. burst_transcr_nonburst_transl. pl

   - Transcriptional burst but no translational burst
   - Exploring impact of mRNA copy number and protein synthesis rates on protein expression noise
   


2. nowaittime_model_parameter_expl.pl 

   - simulating how bursty translation along with bursty transcription impacts protein expression noise 
   - no ribosome demand incorporated 



3. explore_functions.pl 
   
   - Exploring different functions for capturing ribosome demand 



4. parameter_expl2_waittime_model.pl 
   
   - Random sampling of parameter space to estimate the interactions between model parameters and how they influence protein noise 
   - 50 sets of parameters sampled 


5. parameter_expl3_waittime_model.pl 
   
   - systemmatic variation of parameter values 
   - the parameters Kon, Koff, TLon, TLoff, βm varied individually along with variation in βp



6. parameter_expl4_waittime_model.pl 
   
   - systemmatic variation of parameter values 
   - the parameters KHill, A, B, Vmax varied individually along with variation in βp
